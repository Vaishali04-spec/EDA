# Netflix EDA – Business Insights

## Business Problem
Analyze Netflix content data to generate insights that can help Netflix decide:
- What type of shows or movies to produce
- How to expand and grow the business across different countries

The analysis focuses on understanding content trends, genres, ratings, and regional distribution.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains details of TV Shows and Movies available on Netflix.

### Columns
- **show_id**: Unique ID for each Movie or TV Show  
- **type**: Identifier – Movie or TV Show  
- **title**: Title of the Movie or TV Show  
- **director**: Director of the content  
- **cast**: Actors involved  
- **country**: Country where the content was produced  
- **date_added**: Date when the content was added to Netflix  
- **release_year**: Original release year  
- **rating**: TV rating (e.g., TV-MA, PG-13)  
- **duration**: Duration in minutes (Movies) or number of seasons (TV Shows)  
- **listed_in**: Genre(s)  
- **description**: Short summary of the content  

---

## Objectives
- Analyze distribution of Movies vs TV Shows
- Identify popular genres across countries
- Understand content growth over the years
- Analyze country-wise content availability
- Explore rating trends and audience targeting
- Generate insights to support content production and regional strategy

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib / Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## Key Insights (Sample)
- Movies dominate Netflix’s content library compared to TV Shows
- Certain genres perform better in specific regions
- Significant growth in content addition after 2015
- The US and India contribute the highest volume of content
- Mature-rated content forms a large portion of the catalog


## Conclusion
This EDA helps identify content trends and regional preferences, providing data-driven recommendations for Netflix’s content strategy and international expansion.
